Description

Do you love Learning and Development? Are you creative in e-solutions? Do you want to love where you work? Projectline Services is looking for a Learning and Development professional for our global technology client and you will be responsible for crafting, developing, writing and maintaining course materials.

This contract position is an excellent opportunity to be a key part of one of the largest and most transparent technology companies in the world. The right consultant will have deep experience in a classroom and online training. You should have a real passion for technology, a forward-thinking spirit, a deep understanding of modern learning tactics and a get it done attitude.

You will work closely with managers and other trainers to identify specific business needs and to propose training and learning solutions while assessing the needs of target audiences. You will design, develop, write and maintain course materials (i.e. slides, handouts, courseware, lab instructions, lab solutions) that emphasize and positively impact the tasks necessary to perform duties. You will work closely with training managers to define and disseminate best practices for the trainer's area of expertise in both the classroom training and online forums. You have well-rounded experience working with subject matter experts (SMEs) and external technical trainers to present to help incorporate their expertise into various types of training. You have experience researching technology-related information. You maintain awareness of external seminars and training plus literature on available programs.

Skills:

  • 3-5 years’ work-related experience required
  • Experience in instructional design, including needs, learner, context, and task analyses.
  • Experience in developing learning-centered, performance-based learning programs for many different audiences in different environments.
  • Broad experience developing learning programs for classroom and e-learning delivery.
  • Extensive experience in technical communications (writing and editing).
  • Experience working with clients, subject matter experts, and development team members to build learning programs that fit client needs.
  • MS Office experience required.
  • May require experience in multimedia tools to create e-learning solutions.
  • Strong customer service orientation required. Strong knowledge of instructional design methodologies preferred.
  • Bachelor's degree in related field is helpful with some Instructional Design experience. Technical Writing experience is not of equivalent value.

You will be working on 1-3 projects at a time. eLearning projects (procedures, training on tools, how to do certain function, level 100-300 difficulty). You will be working on a project plan. Working with specialists in each project. Meetings on design plan, working on different portions, voice-overs, and Microsoft Teams calls with subject matter experts. Content review with an expert. Sponsor will schedule meetings for the person in the role. They will set up their own cadence with other FTE’s. Working with internal partners only (but some will be sitting in other states/countries).

Tools: Articulate and Adobe Creative Cloud are standard tools. Creating design documents and storyboards for eLearning. It helps to know HTML as well. Familiarity with SuccessFactors (LMS) is a plus.

Work Environment: Will need to be onsite for some training, but they are able to work remotely right away. Occasional onsite work outta to be coordinated with the team; will not have an assigned seat.
