Job Description - Senior Principal Product Manager - Data Connectivity (18001K3K)

Job Description

Senior Principal Product Manager - Data Connectivity-18001K3K   

Preferred Qualifications

 If massive scale, start-up environment; opportunity to drive generational transformation excites you, then read on! If you are a seasoned SaaS business and engineering leader with a focus on SaaS data governance and data management; who believes that data is the currency of an enterprise – this role may be perfect for you!
 
We, at Oracle Corporation, are making huge global investments to deliver the most open, secure, reliable and transformative Data Integration and Data Governance Services to help our customers through their generational shift to the cloud. This is an exciting opportunity as we are rethinking and reengineering our vast array of data integration and data governance software and hardware assets to create the next generation data integration and data governance services. With Oracle’s deeply innovative culture; unique track record of delivering most popular software platforms and its unparalleled ability to maintain a pulse of the enterprise customers – we are the only company on the planet that is delivering the most compelling services at every layer of the cloud.
 
Inside Oracle – we are a fast-growing; fast moving engineering group focused on delivering Oracle’s next generation infrastructure-as-a-service platform. We are on a mission to deliver a massively scalable global infrastructure cloud platform that is a hallmark of innovation, reliability and efficiency. This platform will show our customers the promise of infinite, on-demand, globally scalable infrastructure capacity that they can use to deliver efficient, productive and compliant services to their customers.
 
In this Cloud platform engineering group you be will a primary leader focused on SaaS data and SaaS connectivity within a data integration and data governance context.  Your knack for meticulous and action-focused planning and execution will ensure the our business plans and conversations remain focused. Your ability to work across finance, sales, engineering and marketing organizations seamlessly and effectively will define your success.
 
In this role, your key responsibilities will be as follows –
1. Leadership role in Oracle’s global Data Integration and Data Governance strategy and execution with a focus on SaaS data and SaaS connectivity:
1a. Personal involvement and hands-on leadership with Oracle’s global Data Integration and Data Governance strategy.
1b. Driving day to day execution on connectivity within our new data integration and data governance services as well as providing input on the overall team on the product roadmap.
2. Work as part of an agile development team to define, build and execute on Oracle’s global Data Integration and Data Governance business with engineering and executive leadership teams.
3. Contribute to product, strategy, field, customer, partners, branding and positioning related decisions that stem from global business execution.
4. Contribute to compete positioning and market research for Oracle's Data Integration Data Governance  Services.
5. Contribute to COGs planning, tracking and target definition for Oracle cloud PaaS in partnership with finance.
6. Drive usage, growth, churn, UX analytics across global usage of Oracle’s Data Integration and Data Governance Services.
7. Define campaigns and accelerators based on usage analytics for growth uptake as needed.
8. Drive analytics and business planning for Oracle’s global expansion plans with Cloud.
 
To succeed in this role, you will need to meet following qualifications –
1. Deep understanding of SaaS architecture, SaaS lifecycle, SaaS data architecture, SaaS data governance, SaaS data management, SaaS data security and SaaS data integration.
2. Successfully delivered a cloud service as a key member of the development team.
3. Deep understanding of the cloud service lifecycle and support model.
4. Thorough understanding of data governance, data management, metadata management, business glossaries, data relationships, data provenance, etc.
5. Strong technical acumen; creativity; interpersonal skills; emotional intelligence.
6. 5 years of related SaaS product management experience.
7. 10 years of engineering and business planning experience.
8. Deep understanding of end to end SaaS data integration use cases and how data governance plays a key role in supporting these use cases.
9. Deep understanding of SaaS data architectures for enterprise data governance and enterprise data management.
10. Thorough understanding SaaS data analytics.
11. Deep understanding of data governance markets and use cases.
12. Deep understanding of the global cloud businesses and cloud trends.
13. Penchant for market and business research that drives actions and decisions.
14. Broad financial planning, target setting and tracking experience.
15. Ability drive and derive insights from data and lead broad, cross-group conversations in a matrixed organization.
 
Come over; build your team; make epic things happen and redefine cloud computing! 

Detailed Description and Job Requirements

 Lead a team that acts as the central resource and driving force for the design, process, manufacturing, test, quality and marketing of product(s) as they move from conception to distribution. Organize interdepartmental activities ensuring completion of the project/product on schedule and within budget.

As a member of the product development division, you will specify, design and implement major changes to existing software architecture. Define project needs. Build and execute unit tests and unit test plans. Review integration and regression plans created by QA. Communicate with QA and porting engineering to ensure consistency, testability and portability across products in general.

Provide leadership and expertise in the development of new products/services/processes, frequently operating at the leading edge of technology. Recommends and justifies major changes to existing products/services/processes. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. 8 or more years of software engineering or related experience.
