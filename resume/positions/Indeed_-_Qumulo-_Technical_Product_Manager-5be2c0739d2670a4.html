About the Company
-----------------

Data is the lifeblood of today's businesses. Data represents our knowledge, our creativity, our hard work. Enterprises need their data to scale across the boundaries of traditional data storage to be scalable, manageable, and accessible anywhere and anytime it's needed.

Qumulo offers a scalable file system that runs in the data center and in the public cloud. Founded in 2012 by people who believe that enterprises should be able to seamlessly see, manage, and share their data, Qumulo was built for real customer needs and with the future in mind. Qumulo software and customer support will forever change quality standards in the data storage marketplace.

For more information, visit www.qumulo.com ( http://www.qumulo.com/ ).

Qumulo is a data storage startup in downtown Seattle. We build scalable enterprise data storage that redefines ease of use. Our experienced team of storage experts is looking for a product manager to help us define the first version of our product.

This is an opportunity to join our agile Scrum team as a product owner. Time will be split between developing our understanding of the market and acting as the voice of the customer for our product team. You will live with users and customers, organize our early access product experiments and gather and interpret the feedback. You'll own the backlog and the story prioritization. You'll work with our UX team to define user roles, personae, and organizational archetypes. You'll work with our passionate executive team to keep us focused on the target market. If this sounds like a perfect job then read on to the experience we are looking for!

Required:
  • 5+ years of experience in product management working directly with customers
  • Demonstrated success defining and launching a product
  • Excellent written and verbal communications skills
  • Flexibility and comfort with ambiguity: fail fast, adapt, keep moving
  • Local to Seattle, ability to travel up to 25%
Preferred:
  • 2+ years of experience as Scrum product owner
  • A computer science or engineering degree
  • Delivery of a market disrupting product
  • Enterprise and/or cloud storage experience
