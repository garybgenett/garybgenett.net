Position at Community Brands

Community Brands is a software company that helps organizations achieve social good. Through our technology, we help more than 100,000 associations, nonprofits, schools, faith-based organizations, and partners grow stronger and achieve their missions. Like our clients, our nearly 2,500 employees strive to improve the world in which they live, and we believe that technology plays an important role in that. We are techies and volunteers who dream big and are motivated by more than just a cool job (though that’s a great perk we offer too). The Community Brands culture is one where employees can pursue this passion as part of their profession, and we’re looking for great employees to join us!

Community Brands empowers social impact.  We allow associations, nonprofits, schools, and other membership organizations to succeed faster, grow stronger, and achieve their missions through our purpose-built technology and engagement platforms. 

Job Description

This is a management position for someone who loves technology and is driven to create solutions that provide an exceptional user experience and delight customers. Our suite of SaaS solutions is focused on integrating best-in-class tools to help our more than 4,000 private K-12 school customers thrive in today’s diverse and fast-paced education environment.

In this position you will lead and mentor a team of product managers and product owners.  You will use your technical skills and business savvy to drive revenue growth and achieve corporate business goals. You will also serve as a central communication point internally with our sales and marketing, customer support and engineering teams helping to define and execute a strategic product roadmap. 

Responsibilities include:

  • Oversee the definition and implementation of product roadmaps for a suite of products that could include admission, financial aid, tuition management, payment or student information system (SIS) solutions
  • Evaluate the business return on new feature investments and prioritize features based on multiple criteria
  • Mentor geographically diverse product owners and product managers and help elevate corporate product management processes
  • Perform the product manager role for key, high priority initiatives
  • Communicate road map vision and execution to internal and external communities

 Successful candidates will possess:

  • 5+ years experience as a product manager for a SaaS product
  • The ability to lead, inspire, and elevate direct reports
  • Proven experience solving technology problems along with a high comfort level working with engineering teams using agile/scrum methodologies
  • Demonstrated ability to achieve strategic goals in a fast paced and changing environment
  • Experience and skill working effectively in a matrix management role
  • Proven experience solving technology problems along with a high comfort level working with engineering teams using agile/scrum methodologies
  • An innovative spirit - always looking to find better and new ways to solve problems
  • Superior written and verbal communications skills
  • Lots of energy, a strong entrepreneurial attitude, and excitement about being part of a team
  • MS/MBA, or a BS degree in Computer Science or related engineering field
