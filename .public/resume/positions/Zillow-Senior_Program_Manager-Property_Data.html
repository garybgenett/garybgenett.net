Senior Program Manager, Property Data

 

Zillow is a data-driven organization. The “database of all homes” team is looking for an experienced Program Manager to join our rapidly growing (but still agile) team. We own the systems that process millions of property updates per day, contributing directly to Zillow’s site experience and bottom line.

 
In this role, you will be responsible for:

  • Defining how we incorporate new sources of data
  • Working with the developers to engineer faster and better ways to process data
  • Finding ways to deliver property data to other teams
  • Advising other teams on how to use the property data
  • Communicating the team’s success to executives
  • Working with the support teams to stay ahead of problems
  • Defining acceptance criteria to drive the team’s automated and manual testing efforts
  • Getting stuff shipped!

 

You’ll be able to do amazing things in this role if you are:

  • Highly Analytical. You use data to make decisions and are comfortable gathering it yourself or working with others to gather it.
  • Detail Oriented. Our consumers expect and deserve to receive 100% correct information on Zillow, so you should have the eye for detail that helps ensure that.
  • Results Oriented. You are able to cut to the core of a problem, identify what needs to be done and when, and work with the development team to get solutions shipped.

 

The basic requirements:

  • A least 5 years of technical experience, with at least 3 years in a program management role
  • Experience defining projects, driving requirements, writing detailed functional specifications, and coordinating feature team efforts
  • Enjoy working on back-end systems
  • Strong written and oral communication skills, especially in summarizing and explaining deep technical issues
  • Basic SQL querying ability and comfortable with Excel
  • Experience with AWS, Spark, Kafka, and Hadoop a big plus
