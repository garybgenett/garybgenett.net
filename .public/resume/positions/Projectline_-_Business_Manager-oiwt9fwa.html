Business Manager

Contract Redmond, Washington

Description

Do you want to love where you work? Do you love being organized? Are you passionate about working with technology? Do you love working with creative and collaborative teammates? Projectline Services is looking for a contract Business Manager, to work onsite with our client's Chief of Staff to help support and manage their Partner's Relationship and Rhythm of Business Management requirements. This position will be part of our growing Professional Services team working directly with one of our largest clients in their Redmond office.

Key Responsibilities:

  • You'll handle the team’s internal and external communications and facilitate cross-group and partner collaboration
  • You'll conduct ROB (Rhythm of the Business) activities
  • You will lead executive partner tours and events
  • You'll run Operational Expenses reporting and analysis
  • You will manage the Scorecard
  • You'll manage and support partner investments (review, tracking, and processing Purchase Orders)
  • You'll run readiness & work-back planning for various sales related activities
  • You will orchestrate executive reviews
  • You'll oversee project planning and deliverables with success metrics
  • You will report on work streams statuses
  • You will collaborate within and across groups to build alignment and execute complex projects
  • You will run ad hoc sales support activities

Qualifications and Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business or related four-year degree
  • 5+ years of Program Manager or Business Manager experience
  • Strong project management skills
  • Previous budgeting experience and understanding of financial fundamentals
  • Self-starter with an enthusiastic personality
  • Ability to thrive in a rapidly changing, ambiguous environment with high transparency
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Impeccable attention to detail
  • MS Office skills including Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and SharePoint
  • Previous Microsoft experience preferred
