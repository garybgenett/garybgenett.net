Description

Do you have experience in B2B marketing, program management, project management, or communications? Do you value your independence but want to be part of a collective that will make you proud? Do you want consulting assignments where you can grow and learn while also using your greatest strengths? Then please read on to learn more about Projectline!

We seek to build relationships world's best talent—like you—to put on challenging and rewarding assignments with our hella amazing clients. You deserve world-class benefits and the opportunity to engage with a network of other consultants who are driven, accountable, curious, and who value transparency and partnership. If you would like to connect with an account manager to learn more about our vision for the Future of Work, please submit your resume and a cover letter to let us know the kind of opportunities you are passionate about pursuing.

After completing a hiring assessment and interviewing with us and our clients, if it seems like a good fit, you will get to work as a specialized individual consultant with one of our clients, or part of a complete outsourced team of experts delivering a specific program. You will work closely with your client, sometimes on site, sometimes in a remote location of your choice, and sometimes a blend of remote and onsite work. The assignments vary!

Example consulting roles:
  • B2B Technical Marketing Writer
  • Partner Channel Marketing Manager
  • Marketing Automation Strategist
  • Senior Editor
  • Marketing Project Manager
  • Product Marketing Manager
  • Marketing Automation Architect
  • Internal Executive Communications Manager
  • Video Content Editor
  • Marketo Specialist
  • Content Strategist
Example qualifications:
  • You have 7+ years of experience in marketing, communications, or program/project management
  • You have outstanding written and verbal communication skills with the ability to influence others
  • You are highly organized, flexible, and resourceful
  • You have strong project management fundamentals, both waterfall and agile methodologies
  • You have advanced skills in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, and SharePoint
  • You have previous Microsoft and other high tech marketing experience
